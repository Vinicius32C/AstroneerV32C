SOIL
⤒ BACK TO THE TOP

TIER: SMALL

BYTE VALUE: 100 BYTES | BYTE PER MINUTE: 20 BYTES


SOIL IS A NATURAL RESOURCE THAT CAN BE STORED IN SMALL AND MEDIUM CANISTERS. IT IS THE BUILDING BLOCK OF THE PLANETS, CREATING INTERACTABLE LANDSCAPES. IT IS A FUNDAMENTAL RESOURCE, AS IT IS USED FOR CREATING PLATFORMS, BASES AND FOR EXPLORING.

IT CAN ALSO BE PROCESSED INTO CERTAIN RESOURCES USING A SOIL CENTRIFUGE.

ORGANIC
⤒ BACK TO THE TOP

TIER: SMALL

BYTE VALUE: 50 BYTES | BYTE PER MINUTE: 20 BYTES


ORGANIC IS A NATURAL RESOURCE WHICH APPEARENCE CAN VERY ON THE PLANETS, BUT WILL BE A STACK OF GREEN LEAVES WHEN GATHERED.

IT IS FOUND ON EVERY PLANET WEN USING A TERRAIN TOOL ON MOST SMALLER FLORA.
THE TAPPER SUSTAINABLY PRODUCES ORGANIC OVER TIME.
THE SOIL CENTRIFUGE PRODUCES 8 ORGANIC WITH TWO FULL CANISTERS OF SOIL.

IT IS USED TO:


>CRAFT THINGS ON THE BACKPACK PRINTER, SMELTING FURNACE AND CHEMISTRY LAB.

ASTRONIUM
⤒ BACK TO THE TOP

TIER: SMALL

BYTE VALUE: 500 BYTES | BYTE PER MINUTE: 200 BYTES
