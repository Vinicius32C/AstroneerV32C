ABOUT SYSTEM
SOLAR SYSTEM

THE SOLAR SYSTEM IN ASTRONEER IS SIMILAR TO OURS, AS IN HAVING ONLY ONE STAR AND PLANETS ORBITING IT.

ASTRONEER'S SOLAR SYSTEM'S PLANETS ARE ALL ROCKY, DIFFERENT FROM OURS.

GAME'S SOLAR SYSTEM CONTAINS 5 PLANETS AND 2 MOONS, BUT, BECAUSE OF THEIR NATURE, MOONS ARE CONSIDERED "SATELLITE PLANETS".

EACH PLANET HAS IT'S OWN SELECTION OF RESOURCES, TERRAIN, POWER SUPLY AND HAZARDS, WITH MAKES THE PLAYER TRAVEL TO MOST SO THEY CAN PROGRESS IN ASTRONEER.
